Benny Vazquez-Elvir Bio

I am currently a 2nd year Computer Science student at Illinois Institute of Technology. I have been playing video games and surrounded by technology ever since I was a grade schooler. I adored the amount of fun and the kinds of things you can accomplish with video games and tech. My dream was to become a video game developer but as I grew older, I learned it was much more complicated than that. When I went into High School, I found out that they had a diverse Computer Science curriculum and I jumped at the chance to take as many classes as I could. My love for Computer Science flared after I took so many classes and it naturally led me to major it in college.
I was always considered the smart kid during elementary and it continued up until High School. Due to family issues at home and my motivation dwindling, my grades started to drop. Eventually I met someone that helped me get back on my feet and ever since my junior year of High School, I have been getting straight As and trying as hard as I can to succeed. I have even been trying to start my own business lately with YouTube and Twitch. Also, I was able to get into the Google Computer Science Summer Institute Program which really let me dive more into how awesome CS has been! I hope to work at a big company like Google one day and make a big difference! I am quite the ambitious person and will never back down from a challenge or a chance to learn something new!
